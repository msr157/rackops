About

Infrastructure that
just works

RackOps was built to eliminate the friction between code and production. We believe infrastructure should be boring — reliable, predictable, and automated.

From a single self-hosted server to a multi-region rack fleet, RackOps scales with you without changing how your team works.

  • Self-hosted or cloud — your choice
  • GitOps workflow built in
  • Open source and auditable
  • Zero vendor lock-in
